## 1. Mailchimp CSM (Customer Success Manager)

**Goal:** Add value to customer interactions, identify upsell opportunities, and prevent churn.

### Use Case 1: Account Health Assessment

**Objective:** Quickly understand account status, identify risks, and find opportunities.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Comprehensive Account Overview**
   ```
   Give me a complete account health check. Show me:
   - Total audiences and member counts
   - Campaign activity in the last 30 days
   - Automation usage and performance
   - Any segments or tags being used
   - Overall engagement trends
   ```

2. **Engagement Risk Analysis**
   ```
   Analyze this account for churn risk signals:
   - Show me campaigns with declining open rates
   - Check if they have any paused automations
   - Review audience growth trends (are they losing subscribers?)
   - Identify any unused features (segments, tags, templates)
   ```

3. **Feature Adoption Analysis**
   ```
   What Mailchimp features is this customer using vs. not using?
   - Do they have segments set up? Show me what segments exist
   - Are they using tags? List all tags and member counts
   - Check their template usage - do they have custom templates?
   - What automations are running vs. what could be automated?
   ```

### Use Case 2: Upsell Opportunity Identification

**Objective:** Find opportunities to recommend higher-tier features or additional services.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Automation Opportunity Analysis**
   ```
   This customer only sends regular campaigns. Analyze their sending patterns:
   - What types of emails do they send regularly?
   - Which campaigns could be converted to automations?
   - Show me their campaign frequency - are they sending welcome emails manually?
   - Recommend 3 automation opportunities with business impact
   ```

2. **Segmentation & Personalization Opportunities**
   ```
   Review their segmentation strategy:
   - How many segments do they have? Show me all segments
   - What merge fields are they using? List all merge fields
   - Do they have interest categories set up?
   - Suggest advanced segmentation opportunities based on their member data
   ```

3. **Template & Design Optimization**
   ```
   Analyze their template usage:
   - List all templates they're using
   - Do they have custom-coded templates?
   - Check their campaign content - are they using responsive designs?
   - Suggest template improvements or custom template opportunities
   ```

### Use Case 3: Customer Issue Resolution

**Objective:** Quickly diagnose and resolve customer problems or questions.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Campaign Delivery Investigation**
   ```
   The customer says their last campaign had low opens. Investigate:
   - Show me the campaign report with all metrics
   - Check the send time and day of week
   - Compare to their average campaign performance
   - Review the subject line and content
   - What might explain the low performance?
   ```

2. **Automation Troubleshooting**
   ```
   Customer reports their welcome automation isn't working:
   - List all automations and their status
   - Check if any automations are paused
   - Show me automation email details
   - Identify what might be wrong
   ```

3. **Data Validation**
   ```
   Customer questions their subscriber count. Verify:
   - Show me total audience size
   - Break down by member status (subscribed, unsubscribed, cleaned, pending)
   - Show recent activity (new subscribers, unsubscribes)
   - Explain any discrepancies
   ```

## 2. Marketers Reporting to Executives

**Goal:** Demonstrate marketing performance, provide data-backed insights, and show strategic mastery.

### Use Case 1: Performance Reporting & Analytics

**Objective:** Create comprehensive reports that demonstrate ROI and performance.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Quarterly Performance Report**
   ```
   Create a comprehensive Q4 2024 performance report:
   - List all campaigns sent in Q4 with full metrics
   - Calculate average open rate, click rate, and unsubscribe rate
   - Identify top 5 and bottom 5 performing campaigns
   - Compare Q4 performance to Q3 (if available)
   - Highlight key insights and trends
   ```

2. **Campaign Performance Benchmarking**
   ```
   Analyze our campaign performance this year:
   - What's our average open rate across all campaigns?
   - Which campaign types perform best (regular, automation, etc.)?
   - Show me campaigns that exceeded our average by 20%+
   - What do our top performers have in common?
   - Create a performance benchmark report
   ```

3. **ROI & Engagement Analysis**
   ```
   Show me the business impact of our email marketing:
   - Total campaigns sent this quarter
   - Total subscribers reached
   - Overall engagement rates (opens, clicks)
   - If we have e-commerce data, show revenue impact
   - Calculate engagement trends over time
   ```

### Use Case 2: Strategic Insights & Recommendations

**Objective:** Provide data-driven recommendations for improving marketing performance.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Optimization Opportunities**
   ```
   Identify optimization opportunities:
   - Show me campaigns with below-average performance
   - What patterns do underperforming campaigns share?
   - Analyze send times - when do we get best engagement?
   - Review subject line patterns in top vs. bottom performers
   - Recommend 5 specific improvements
   ```

2. **Audience Growth Strategy**
   ```
   Analyze our audience growth and engagement:
   - Show audience growth trends over the last 6 months
   - Break down by member status (new, unsubscribed, cleaned)
   - Which segments have highest engagement?
   - What's our subscriber retention rate?
   - Recommend audience growth strategies
   ```

3. **Content Performance Analysis**
   ```
   What content resonates with our audience?
   - Show me campaigns sorted by click rate
   - Analyze campaign content themes
   - Which merge fields or personalization elements are we using?
   - Review template usage - which templates perform best?
   - Recommend content strategy improvements
   ```

### Use Case 3: Executive Dashboard & Quick Insights

**Objective:** Provide quick, high-level insights for executive briefings.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Monthly Executive Summary**
   ```
   Create an executive summary for this month:
   - Total campaigns sent
   - Total subscribers
   - Key performance metrics (open rate, click rate)
   - Top performing campaign
   - Biggest opportunity for improvement
   - One-sentence recommendation
   ```

2. **Campaign Performance Snapshot**
   ```
   Give me a quick snapshot of our last 10 campaigns:
   - Campaign name and send date
   - Open rate and click rate
   - Performance vs. average (above/below)
   - One insight per campaign
   ```

3. **Strategic Health Check**
   ```
   Quick health check for executive review:
   - Are we growing or shrinking our list?
   - Is engagement improving or declining?
   - What's our biggest risk right now?
   - What's our biggest opportunity?
   - One strategic recommendation
   ```

## 3. Business Owners

**Goal:** Quickly understand marketing performance, make informed decisions, and stay aligned with marketing efforts.

### Use Case 1: Quick Status Updates

**Objective:** Get fast answers about what's happening in marketing.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **What's Working This Month?**
   ```
   Give me a simple summary of our email marketing this month:
   - How many campaigns did we send?
   - What's our average performance?
   - What's working well?
   - What needs improvement?
   - Keep it simple and actionable
   ```

2. **Recent Campaign Activity**
   ```
   What did my marketing team send in the last 2 weeks?
   - List all campaigns with dates
   - Show performance (good, average, or needs work)
   - Highlight the best performer
   - Any concerns I should know about?
   ```

3. **List Health Check**
   ```
   Quick check on our email list:
   - How many subscribers do we have?
   - Are we growing or shrinking?
   - What's our engagement rate?
   - Is our list healthy?
   ```

### Use Case 2: ROI & Business Impact

**Objective:** Understand the business value of email marketing.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **Marketing ROI Assessment**
   ```
   Show me the business value of our email marketing:
   - How many people are we reaching?
   - What's our engagement rate?
   - If we have e-commerce connected, show sales impact
   - Are we getting good return on our Mailchimp investment?
   - What's the cost per engaged subscriber?
   ```

2. **Customer Engagement Analysis**
   ```
   Who are our most engaged email subscribers?
   - Show me engagement patterns
   - Are engaged subscribers also our best customers?
   - If we have store data, connect email engagement to purchases
   - What does this tell us about our customer relationships?
   ```

3. **Growth & Scale Assessment**
   ```
   Can we scale our email marketing?
   - Show me our current list size and growth rate
   - What's our capacity for growth?
   - Are we maximizing our current subscriber base?
   - What would it take to double our email impact?
   ```

### Use Case 3: Strategic Decision Making

**Objective:** Get recommendations for marketing strategy and investments.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **What Should We Focus On Next?**
   ```
   Based on all our email data, what should we focus on next month?
   - What's our biggest opportunity?
   - What's our biggest risk?
   - What's one thing we should start doing?
   - What's one thing we should stop doing?
   - Give me 3 specific recommendations
   ```

2. **Marketing Investment Justification**
   ```
   Help me understand if we're getting value from Mailchimp:
   - Show me key metrics that matter for our business
   - Are we using the platform effectively?
   - What features are we paying for but not using?
   - What would we miss if we stopped using Mailchimp?
   - Is this a good investment?
   ```

3. **Strategic Growth Planning**
   ```
   I'm thinking about expanding our email marketing. What does our data say?
   - What's our current capacity?
   - What's our engagement potential?
   - What would need to change to scale?
   - Show me the data to support expansion
   - What are the risks?
   ```

## Tips for Using These Prompts

1. **Start Broad, Then Narrow:** Begin with overview questions, then drill into specifics
2. **Use Follow-up Questions:** Claude can answer follow-ups based on initial responses
3. **Combine Multiple Tools:** These prompts leverage multiple MCP tools automatically
4. **Customize for Your Needs:** Modify prompts to match your specific business context
5. **Save Useful Prompts:** Keep a library of prompts that work well for your use case

## Available Tools Reference

The Mailchimp MCP provides access to:

**Read Tools:**
- Audiences, Members, Segments, Tags
- Campaigns, Reports, Content
- Automations, Templates
- E-commerce (Stores, Products, Orders, Customers)
- Files, Folders
- Account information

**Write Tools:**
- Create/Update/Delete Campaigns
- Create/Update Templates
- Upload Files
- Generate & Upload AI Images
- Send Campaigns (with confirmation)

All prompts above are designed to work with these available tools.
